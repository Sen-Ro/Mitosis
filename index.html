What is Mitosis and why is it so important?
Mitosis is the process by which a single eukaryotic cell divides to produce two genetically identical daughter cells. Cytokinesis is the final step of cell division, where the cell splits into two. Without mitosis you wouldn't be able to grow, heal wounds and develop.
